Our Mission

We're a team of high schoolers from the Bay Area with a passion for machine learning. One thing we know for certain is that it's going to shape the future. Our course is designed to be simple, free, and concise, giving you the tools you need to understand the underlying concepts of AI without feeling overwhelmed. We want to make AI accessible to everyone, especially students like us, and help you get started on your journey into the world of machine learning.
